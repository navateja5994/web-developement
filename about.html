About Us

Mother theresa Engineering College, established in 1998, is one of the premier institutions for engineering education in the country. We are committed to providing a dynamic learning environment that promotes academic excellence, innovative research, and community engagement.

Mission

To provide quality technical education and foster innovation through cutting-edge research and collaboration with industry and society.

Vision

To be a globally recognized institution that shapes engineers and leaders who will transform society and industry with integrity and purpose.

  • Electronics and Communication Engineering
